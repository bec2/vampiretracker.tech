Highgate Cemetery, London

n the 60s and 70s, there were a number of sightings of a creature with red eyes that could dissipate into smoke, all focusing around this London cemetery. As a result, said cemetery became a popular location for paranormal reporters and researchers.

One such vampire hunter had legal action taken against him, which put people off the trail for some time.

In 2012, however, there have been reports of a pale figure dressed in a suit wandering this area. Apparently this vampire just needed some ‘me time’ for a few decades.

This one sounds awfully like an anime I watched one time.
