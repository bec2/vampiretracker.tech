Glen Park Road, Birmingham

This Brummie vampire reportedly attacked locals in 2005. It started with stories of a man being bitten as he walked down the street and his attacker pouncing on heroic passersby who sought to aid the victim. One woman reportedly received a bite to the hand.

Following this event, multiple reports began to pop up around the area - vampire attacks in Small Health, Alum Rock, and Saltley. Apparently this vampire wasn’t allergic to salt circles. Police were unconvinced by tales of a serial biter, remarking that they were likely looking at an ‘urban myth’ gone wild.

A Small Heath preacher, Father Rohan, remarked that he was not scared, as he had a lot of crucifixes in his house.
